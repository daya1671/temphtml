Pharmacy Billing & Optimization Services

Maximize reimbursement revenue for your pharmacy.

Our pharmacy billing optimization services offer independent pharmacies an alternative to Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBMs). Quadruple your reimbursements for workers compensation, no fault, personal injury and auto prescriptions. StreamlineRx provides pharmacies guidance on optimizing medications, then bills insurance companies directly on behalf of the pharmacy. Ultimately alleviating the need for a billing department and offers full transparency of actually billings, collections and reimbursements.

Are you filling prescriptions for Workers Compensation, Personal Injury and Auto Injury patients? StreamlineRx Billing Optimization Services can dramatically increase revenue by:
  • Providing guidance to optimize your formulary and substitution protocols leveraging high-margin medications.
  • Providing end-to-end service from contacting adjusters, getting pre-approvals through collections and reimbursement to you. Our dedicated in-house Revenue Cycle Management team are experts in Work Comp and Personal Injury billing.
  • Providing direct billing to insurance companies bypassing the PBMs. We have working relationships will ALL Work Comp insurance companies and will get you the maximum reimbursement on your prescription dispenses.

Don't support the PBMs and allow them to take the lions share of your reimbursements. Work with StreamlineRx to maximize your reimbursements!

Pharmacy Billing Optimization Services Feature:

Immediate and easy adjudication on all workers compensation, no fault, personal injury and auto prescriptions.

  • Acknowledgement of claim receipt and access to track claim though Provider Portal software.
  • Monthly Reports providing transparency on prescriptions submitted, billed and collected.
  • Monthly payments made to pharmacy.
  • Guidance on medication options to optimize revenue.
  • Up to 7 times greater reimbursement per script.
